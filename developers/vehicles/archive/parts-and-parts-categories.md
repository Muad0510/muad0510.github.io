# Parts & Parts Categories

These are the part names used in filenames for .mesh and .ent files. Each header is their category - `base` is used in both `Interior` & `Exterior`.

### Interior

* `base`
* `steering_wheel`
* `pedals`

### Exterior

* `base`
* `bumper_f`
* `bumper_b`&#x20;
* `hood`
* `trunk`
* `door_fl`
* `door_fr`
* `door_bl`
* `door_br`
* `fuelcap`
* `fuelport`&#x20;

### Wheel

* `wheel`
